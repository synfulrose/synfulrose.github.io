Movie Ranking project

The Best Movies According to me

  1. The Godfather (1972)

    Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this crime drama is often cited as one of the greatest films of all time. It stars Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, and James Caan and follows the powerful Corleone crime family.

  2. Citizen Kane (1941)

    Directed by Orson Welles, this groundbreaking film is celebrated for its innovative cinematography and storytelling techniques. It examines the life of a media mogul and is frequently regarded as a masterpiece of cinema.

  3. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

    Directed by Frank Darabont, this drama based on a Stephen King novella is highly acclaimed for its compelling story and outstanding performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman.


The 3 Worst movies, like ever!

  1. Plan 9 from Outer Space (1959)

    Often referred to as one of the worst movies of all time, this sci-fi horror film directed by Ed Wood has gained a cult following for its unintentionally humorous and low-budget production.

  2. Battlefield Earth (2000)

    Based on a novel by L. Ron Hubbard, this sci-fi film starring John Travolta has been panned for its convoluted story, over-the-top acting, and questionable special effects.

  3. Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen (2009)

    Although commercially successful, this Michael Bay-directed film is often criticized for its confusing plot, excessive CGI, and juvenile humor.
